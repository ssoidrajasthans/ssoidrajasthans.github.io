SSO ID Rajasthan

SSO ID Rajasthan Portal

Single Sign-On (SSO) ID Rajasthan is a digital identity that allows citizens, businesses, and government employees to access multiple Rajasthan government services using one login.

What is SSO ID Rajasthan?

SSO ID Rajasthan is an initiative by the Government of Rajasthan to simplify access to online services. Through the official portal sso.rajasthan.gov.in, users can log in once and access multiple government departments without creating separate usernames and passwords.

The SSO ID system is designed to improve transparency, reduce paperwork, and promote digital governance across Rajasthan.

Types of SSO ID Rajasthan

  • Citizen SSO ID – For residents of Rajasthan
  • Udhyog SSO ID – For business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Government Employee SSO ID – For state government employees

How to Register for SSO ID Rajasthan

  1. Visit the official website: sso.rajasthan.gov.in
  2. Click on the Register button
  3. Select your user type (Citizen / Udhyog / Government Employee)
  4. Register using one of the following:
    • Jan Aadhaar
    • Bhamashah Card
    • Google Account
    • Aadhaar (via OTP)
  5. Fill in personal details and create your SSO ID
  6. Submit the form and complete verification

SSO ID Rajasthan Login Process

  1. Go to sso.rajasthan.gov.in
  2. Enter your SSO ID, Password, and Captcha
  3. Click on the Login button
  4. After login, access all linked government services

Services Available on SSO Portal

  • e-Mitra Services
  • Rajasthan Employment Portal
  • Bhamashah & Jan Aadhaar Services
  • ePDS (Ration Card)
  • Scholarship Services
  • Rajasthan Sampark
  • Pay Manager
  • IFMS & HRMS
  • Business Registration Services
  • Digital Certificates

Forgot SSO ID or Password?

If you forget your SSO ID or password, follow these steps:

  1. Open the SSO login page
  2. Click on Forgot Password
  3. Enter registered mobile number or email
  4. Verify using OTP
  5. Reset your password successfully

Benefits of SSO ID Rajasthan

  • Time-saving and paperless process
  • Easy access to welfare schemes
  • 24×7 online availability

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Is SSO ID mandatory in Rajasthan?
Yes, SSO ID is mandatory to access most Rajasthan government online services.

Q2. Can I create multiple SSO IDs?
No, one person should have only one SSO ID.

Q3. Is SSO ID free?
Yes, registration and usage of SSO ID is completely free.

Q4. Can non-residents apply for SSO ID?
Citizen SSO ID is mainly for Rajasthan residents, but business users can register under Udhyog SSO.
